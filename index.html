Change Up

Personal financial management
------------------------------------
Angular.JS - Ruby on Rails - PostgresSQL - D3.js - SSL

Change up is a quick and secure financial tracker, that allows you to monitor all of your accounts, and visualize spending habits in a clear, concise way. Change up was built by myself and a partner in three weeks at GA. Using D3 and SQL queries the user can see the average amount spent in a day, month, week, or even see where and what they spent the most money on. Change up also has a budget calculator for each category of expenditures allow the setting of goals

Built with: Angular.JS, D3.js, Ruby on Rails, PostgresSQL, Thin SSL, Bootstrap.
